Welcome to easy money

Map out your money goals

Create Savers for all your money goals, commitments, bills, a home...whatever! Every saver can have its own name, emoji and target amount so you always know where you stand.

2Up

2Up is a new way to fight finances together in co-op mode. Easily invite your Player 2 and join forces to battle bills, save for shared goals, share expenses and track spending.

Budgeting

Put your budget on cruise control. Say goodbye to nasty surprises, know where every buck goes and how much you can spend.

Pay Day

Use your Up account to receive your salary and pay your bills. Pay Day will give you a "spendable balance" so you better know what you can safely spend before you get paid next. Let us do the math.

Never saved $1000?

Dream of having a grand on hand? Save Up $1000 will help you get there by setting a simple weekly savings challenge. Each week you’ll pick one of a few amounts to save. Some big. Some small. We’ll stash your cash safely away so it will stack up (though if you need it, that’s OK: just close your Challenge).

Pay Splitting

Pay Splitting lets you carve up your pay into a bunch of different Savers as soon as it hits your account, and before you get a chance to spend it. An easier way to stockpile for your lifestyle. Whether you're paid the same amount each time or not, you can instantly carve up your payday cash and choose your own savings adventure.
